DraWit is a site mainly designed for drawing, which can be done on the canvas found under 'draw'. The function of each individual button is explained below. The account and share functions will be implemented in DraWit version 2.0 (byowa).
This button sets the color you are drawing with.
After clicking you can type the name or rgb value of the required color.
For a list of all colors click here.
This button sets the pen size.
After clicking you can typ the required pen size.
This button clears the canvas.
Don't click it by accident.
This button lets you upload a picture as background.
After clicking you can choose a picture from your pc.
With this button you can download your painting.
After clicking you can find your painting in the map downloads
This button sets the size of the canvas.
You can type the width in the first bar and the height in the second.
This button lets you change the tool.
After clicking you can choose between a pencil and an eraser.
